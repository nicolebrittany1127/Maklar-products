How AuthAlly Works

From clinical note to authorization-ready in seconds.

AuthAlly doesn't replace your clinical team — it makes their documentation payer-proof before it leaves your facility.

01

Upload Your Documentation

Paste text or upload a PDF directly from Kipu, TIER, or any EHR. AuthAlly reads progress notes, H&Ps, assessments, and discharge summaries in seconds.

02

Select Payer and Level of Care

Choose from 14 payers and 10 levels of care. AuthAlly applies the exact criteria framework — ASAM or InterQual — that payer uses for that specific level of care.

03

Review Gaps and Submit with Confidence

AuthAlly surfaces missing criteria and drafts clinical language to address each gap. Your team adds it to the note, then submits — ready for approval.

Platform Features

Everything you need to stop denials before they happen.

AuthAlly brings clinical intelligence and payer-specific criteria into one platform built specifically for behavioral health and addiction treatment.

🧠

ASAM Dimensional Screening

Screens progress notes against all 6 ASAM dimensions for the selected level of care, identifying gaps that commonly trigger denials.

📋

Payer-Specific Criteria Engine

Each payer uses different criteria — some use ASAM, others InterQual, some hybrid. AuthAlly applies the right framework automatically.

✍️

AI Remediation Drafting

Doesn't just flag gaps — drafts the clinical language needed to address each gap. Providers add it to the note in under two minutes.

📄

PDF Upload from Any EHR

Upload directly from Kipu, TIER, or any EHR-exported PDF. No copy-paste required. AuthAlly extracts and analyzes automatically.

📊

Exportable Gap Reports

Print-ready authorization gap reports for prior auth submissions or denial appeals, including dimensional assessment and payer-specific findings.

🔒

HIPAA Compliant

Built to meet healthcare data privacy and security standards. All clinical documentation handled with enterprise-grade security.
