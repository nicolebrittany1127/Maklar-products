Platform Features

Everything you need to stay credentialed and compliant.

Creddi brings structure and clarity to a process that's historically been scattered across spreadsheets and email threads.

Centralized Provider Profiles

House all provider licenses, certifications, DEA registrations, malpractice history, and credentials in a single, organized profile — always audit-ready.

Recredentialing Timeline Tracking

Never miss a deadline. Creddi automatically tracks credentialing and recredentialing cycles, surfacing upcoming expirations weeks in advance.

Payer Network Status Management

Track enrollment and participation status across all contracted payers. Know exactly where every provider stands in the network — in real time.

Provider Roster Management

Manage your entire roster with ease. Add, update, and organize providers across multiple locations, specialties, and payer relationships.

Automated Deadline Alerts

Get notified before licenses lapse or credentialing windows close. Configurable alerts keep your team proactive rather than reactive.

Reporting & Compliance Dashboards

Generate instant status reports for internal reviews, audits, or payer submissions. Get a clear picture of your credentialing health at a glance.

How It Works

Up and running in four simple steps.

Creddi is designed to be intuitive for credentialing professionals from day one.

1

Import Your Roster

Upload your provider roster directly into Creddi — from a spreadsheet or manual entry. We organize everything automatically.

2

Build Provider Profiles

Attach licenses, certifications, DEA numbers, malpractice coverage, and payer enrollment data to each provider.

3

Set Your Timelines

Define credentialing and recredentialing cycles. Creddi calculates upcoming deadlines and sends automated alerts.

4

Monitor & Report

Use your dashboard to track compliance in real time and generate reports whenever you need them.
