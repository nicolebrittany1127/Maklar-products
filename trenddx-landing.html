Platform Features

Every RCM metric that matters, in one place.

TrendDX turns complex revenue cycle data into clear, actionable insights your entire team can act on — without needing a data analyst.

Real-Time KPI Dashboard

Monitor your most critical RCM metrics live — clean claim rates, denial rates, days in AR, collection rates, and more — updated in real time as your data flows in.

Trend Analysis & Benchmarking

Track performance over time with visual trend lines. Compare current metrics against historical baselines and industry benchmarks to understand where you stand.

Denial Tracking & Root Cause

Identify denial patterns by payer, denial code, provider, and service line. Surface root causes early so your team can resolve issues before they impact revenue.

AR Aging & Collections Visibility

Get a clear view of your accounts receivable by aging bucket, payer, and provider. Prioritize follow-up where it matters most and reduce outstanding balances faster.

Custom Reports & Exports

Build and schedule custom reports for leadership, payers, or internal teams. Export in multiple formats and share insights without manual data wrangling.

Threshold Alerts & Notifications

Set performance thresholds for any KPI. When metrics fall outside acceptable ranges, TrendDX alerts your team automatically — before small issues become big problems.
