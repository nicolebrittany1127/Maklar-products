How AuthAlly works

From clinical note to
authorization-ready in seconds

AuthAlly doesn't replace your clinical team — it makes their documentation payer-proof before it leaves your facility.

01

Upload your documentation

Paste text or upload a PDF directly from Kipu, TIER, or any EHR. AuthAlly reads progress notes, H&Ps, assessments, and discharge summaries in seconds.

02

Select payer and level of care

Choose from 14 payers and 10 levels of care. AuthAlly applies the exact criteria framework — ASAM or InterQual — that payer uses for that specific level of care and diagnosis type.

03

Get your gap report and fix it

Receive an authorization readiness score, dimensional assessment, and AI-drafted remediation language — specific clinical sentences your team can add to the note in minutes.

Features

Everything your UR team needs.
Nothing they don't.

⚖️

MH vs SUD framework detection

Auto-detects whether ASAM or InterQual applies based on primary diagnosis. Critical for Sunshine Health and others that use different frameworks for MH vs substance use.

🔄

Concurrent stay review

Compare your initial auth note against a current progress note to identify documentation gaps before submitting for continued stay reauthorization.

📋

Batch review

Analyze up to 8 patient documents simultaneously with a summary dashboard. Ideal for high-volume UR teams managing multiple concurrent authorizations.

✍️

AI remediation drafting

AuthAlly drafts the specific clinical language your provider needs to add — written in the style of a progress note addendum, ready to copy and paste directly.

📄

PDF upload from any EHR

Upload directly from Kipu, TIER, or any EHR-exported PDF. No copy-paste required. AuthAlly extracts clinical information and analyzes it automatically.

📊

Exportable gap reports

Print-ready authorization gap reports you can attach to prior auth submissions or denial appeals, including dimensional assessment and payer-specific findings.
